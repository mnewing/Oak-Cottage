About our Cottage

Our lovely 3 bedroomed cottage is the perfect get away for all the family. The cottage is set in a traditaionly cottage garden with large lawn for games or taking tea. Downstairs there is a big kitchen dinner and a large sitting room with a open fireplace perefct for cosie winter retreats.

Upstairs there are three generous bedrooms and 1 family bathroom. One bedroom has a double , one has bunk beds and the other twin beds. There is a drying cupboard on the ground floor boots and coats for those keen walkers.

The cottage overlooks the dale and beautiful unintruputed views to sea. It is only a 10 min walk via a pretty off road path to the beach. We welcome well behaved dogs.

About the area

Tyddyn Llan is well placed to explore the magnificence of Snowdonia and its many castles and monuments. The Roman City of Chester is only 35 miles away, as are the majestic Mawddach and Dyfi estuaries leading to fine sandy beaches and rocky coves.

Walking from the hotel, via ancient routes and old driving roads, experience the peace and quiet of this unspoilt landscape. Our local and highly qualified guide is also avalable.
